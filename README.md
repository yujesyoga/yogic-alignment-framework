## Thesis

The classical yogic corpus — Patañjali's Yoga Sūtras, the Bhagavad Gītā, and the Haṭha Yoga tradition — constitutes the most complete alignment meta-system available for artificial intelligence. Not as metaphor. As architecture.

## Three Contributions

1. **For today:** A formal mapping of yogic principles to agentic AI design — immediately applicable.
2. **For tomorrow:** A structural argument for why ontological frameworks will outperform behavioral conditioning as AI approaches general intelligence.
3. **For the deeper question:** A proposition that any genuinely conscious entity may require not just alignment constraints, but a path toward self-understanding.
